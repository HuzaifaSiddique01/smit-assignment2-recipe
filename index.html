CHINESE SOUP

  1. Prep: 5 mins

  2. Cook: 10 mins

  3. Total: 15 mins

  4. Servings: 3 to 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken broth or stock (or vegetable broth)
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • Salt, to taste
  • A few drops sesame oil, optional
  • 1 to 2 spring onions, finely chopped, garnish

Steps to Make It:

01: Gather the ingredients.

02: In a wok or saucepan, bring the 4 cups of chicken broth or
stock to a boil. Very slowly, pour in the lightly beaten eggs in
a steady stream.

03: To make shreds, stir the egg rapidly in a clockwise direction
as you pour for 1 minute. To make thin streams or ribbons,
gently stir the eggs in a clockwise direction until they form.

04: Add the pepper, salt, and sesame oil if using. Pour into bowls
and garnish with spring onion.
